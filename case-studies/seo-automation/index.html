How Programmatic SEO Works With AI

Programmatic SEO is one of the most effective growth strategies in digital marketing ‚Äî and one of the hardest to execute. The traditional approach requires a developer to build template systems, a data engineer to manage the data pipeline, and a content strategist to ensure quality. Most startups can't afford the $5,000‚Äì$15,000 upfront build, let alone the ongoing maintenance.

Our AI agent eliminates all of that. Here's how it works:

  • Template generation: The agent creates HTML templates with dynamic placeholders for location names, service descriptions, local details, and CTAs.
  • Data pipeline: The agent builds and maintains data.json files ‚Äî structured datasets containing city information, integration details, local statistics, and unique content for each page variant.
  • Page generation: Template + data = pages at scale. The agent combines templates with data to produce unique, high-quality landing pages ‚Äî each with custom content, not just find-and-replace city names.
  • Quality control: Every generated page includes unique introductory copy, locally relevant details, proper structured data, and internal links. No thin content. No duplicate content penalties.
